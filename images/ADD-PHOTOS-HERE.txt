Drop photo files into this folder with these exact names:

  library-exterior.jpg   — Outside of the building (front or side view)
  library-interior.jpg   — Inside shot (shelves, reading area, oak woodwork)
  library-reading.jpg    — People reading, or a cozy corner of the library
  library-childrens.jpg  — The children's section (west side of the building)

Tips:
- JPG format works best for photos
- Aim for at least 800px wide for good quality
- Good sources: the library's Facebook page, photos staff have taken,
  or ask Christy if she has any on file
- Right-click any photo on the Facebook page → Save image as →
  rename it to one of the names above → drop it in this folder
