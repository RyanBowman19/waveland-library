Our Commitments

Policies at a Glance

The Waveland-Brown Township Public Library is governed by its Board of Trustees, which reviews and adopts policies at its monthly meetings (last Tuesday of each month — see our Events page). Full policy documents are available at the library; summaries of our key policies appear below.

🪪

Library Cards

Waveland and Brown Township residents receive a free library card — a policy in place since the library opened in 1915. Bring proof of residence (such as a photo ID or piece of mail) to sign up at the front desk.

🔄

Borrowing & Returns

Most materials check out for a standard loan period and may be renewed if no one else is waiting. Items may be returned at the front desk or arranged through our curbside service. Please call 765-435-2700 with questions about due dates, renewals, or holds.

💻

Internet & Computer Use

Public computers and Wi-Fi are available free of charge. Users are expected to use library technology responsibly and in accordance with our internet use policy, available at the front desk.

🏛️

Meeting Room Use

Our downstairs community room is available to local groups and organizations. Contact the library to check availability and review meeting room guidelines.

🤝

Patron Conduct

The library is a welcoming space for everyone. We ask all patrons to be considerate of others so that the library remains a place where families and friends can gather comfortably.

📋

Public Records & Board Meetings

Board of Trustees meetings are open to the public and held in the library on the last Tuesday of every month. Meeting minutes and budget documents are available upon request.

📄 For complete, current policy documents, please visit the library or call 765-435-2700.
