Services

Here to help — in the library, at the curb, or at your door.

🚗 Curbside Pickup & Book Delivery: To arrange curbside pickup or schedule a book delivery, please call 765-435-2700.
🪪

Free Library Cards

Waveland and Brown Township residents receive a free library card — a tradition since 1915. Stop in with proof of residence to sign up.

📚

Books & Materials

Fiction, nonfiction, children's books, large print, magazines, DVDs, and audiobooks — plus access to materials from libraries across Indiana through our shared catalog.

🚗

Curbside Pickup

Place a hold or call ahead, and we'll bring your items right out to your car. Just call 765-435-2700 when you arrive.

🏠

Book Delivery

Unable to visit the library? Call us to schedule a book delivery and we'll bring the library to you.

💻

Public Computers & Wi-Fi

Free public computers and wireless internet for browsing, email, job applications, and homework.

📱

E-Books & Audiobooks

Borrow digital books and audiobooks from home with your library card. Ask our staff to help you get set up on your device.

🖨️

Printing & Copying

Printing and photocopying services are available at the front desk for a small fee.

🏛️

Community Meeting Room

Our downstairs meeting room has hosted movies, story hours, and community presentations for over a century. Ask about availability for your group.

🧒

Children's Programs

Story hours and youth programming in the children's section — in the same west wing where kids have gathered since 1915.

Questions about any service? Call us at 765-435-2700 or stop by — we're happy to help.
