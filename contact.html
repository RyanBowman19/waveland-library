Finding Us

Our Historic Building

You can't miss us — we're still in the original 1915 Carnegie building, with its handsome brick exterior and the bronze tablet in the entryway reading “Gift from Andrew Carnegie.”

Up the stairs to the east you'll find the adult collection; the children's section is to the west, just as it has been for over a century. The community meeting room is downstairs.
